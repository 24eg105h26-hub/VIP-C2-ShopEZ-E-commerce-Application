- Product Catalog Management
- Order Processing & Fulfillment
- Payment Processing
- Real-time Notifications
- Search & Filtering
- Review Management
- Seller Onboarding

### 3. **Key Resources**
- Development Team
- Cloud Infrastructure
- Database (MongoDB)
- Payment Processing APIs
- WebSocket Infrastructure (Socket.io)
- Email Service
- File Storage Service

### 4. **Value Propositions**

#### For Customers
✨ **Convenience**: One-stop shopping experience
✨ **Variety**: Multiple sellers, diverse products
✨ **Security**: Safe payment options
✨ **Speed**: Fast search and checkout
✨ **Trust**: Reviews and ratings
✨ **Flexibility**: Wishlist and cart management

#### For Sellers
✨ **Easy Setup**: Quick seller onboarding
✨ **Inventory Control**: Efficient variant management
✨ **Scalability**: Reach more customers
✨ **Analytics**: Sales insights and metrics
✨ **Support**: Dedicated seller dashboard

#### For Administrator
✨ **Control**: Full platform oversight
✨ **Insights**: User and sales analytics
✨ **Efficiency**: Automated workflows
✨ **Reliability**: System monitoring and alerts

### 5. **Customer Segments**
- **Retail Customers**: Age 18-65
- **Small Business Sellers**: 1-100 employees
- **Platform Administrators**: Internal team

### 6. **Channels**
- Web Platform (Desktop)

- Admin Dashboard

### 7. **Customer Relationships**
- Self-service browsing
- Email support

- Seller support portal
- Admin communication

### 8. **Revenue Streams**
- Commission on Seller Sales (5-10%)
- Premium Seller Features

### 9. **Cost Structure**

- Support Staff

## Problem Statement

### **Primary Problem**
"Small sellers and businesses struggle to find an affordable, easy-to-use online marketplace solution that doesn't compromise on features or security, while customers seek a trustworthy platform with diverse products and secure transactions."

### **Impact Assessment**
- **Severity**: High
- **Frequency**: Daily (millions of transactions globally)
- **User Base Affected**: Billions worldwide
- **Current Market Gap**: Large

## Empathy Mapping

### **Customer Persona**

#### SAYS
- "I want to find products quickly"
- "I need to trust the sellers"
- "Checkout should be fast"
- "I want to track my order in real-time"

#### THINKS
- "Is this website secure?"
- "Will my payment be safe?"
- "Can I trust this seller?"
- "How fast will delivery be?"

#### FEELS
- Anxious about online payments
- Excited about new products
- Frustrated with slow search
- Happy with good deals

#### DOES
- Browses products regularly
- Compares prices
- Reads reviews
- Shares recommendations

#### PAINS
- Complex navigation
- Unclear product information
- Slow checkout process
- Poor customer support

#### GAINS
- Fast delivery
- Good prices
- Quality products
- Easy returns
- Reliable sellers

---

### **Seller Persona**

#### SAYS
- "I need easy inventory management"
- "I want to reach more customers"
- "I need sales insights"
- "Can you help me grow?"

#### THINKS
